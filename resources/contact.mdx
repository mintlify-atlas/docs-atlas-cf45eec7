Left-Sided Studios is reachable through several official channels. Discord is the primary and fastest way to contact the team for general questions, community involvement, or real-time updates. For formal or business inquiries, the website contact form is also available.

<Note>
  LSS is a small, independent three-person team. Response times may vary depending on current development cycles.
</Note>

## Email

For partnerships, press, or business matters, email the studio directly at **leftsidedstudios@gmail.com**. The contact form on the LSS website also routes submissions to this address — fill in your name, email, subject, and message and it will be delivered to the team's inbox.

<Note>
  For press inquiries, the [Press Kit](/resources/press-kit) page also provides downloadable brand assets, the official studio bio, and direct contact guidance.
</Note>
