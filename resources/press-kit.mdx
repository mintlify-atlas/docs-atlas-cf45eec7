## Studio bio

> Left-Sided Studios is an independent collective specializing in narrative-heavy action and simulation titles, utilizing a network of labels to deliver unique interactive experiences.

Founded in 2023 by three co-founders — Vermetra, DaRealSansYT, and RocketBlasts — the studio operates a network of subsidiary labels (ANdE Studios, Gotch-ya Studios, Bomb Banana Studios, Endgame Studios) and maintains a strategic partnership with Citadel Studios. All active projects are developed in Godot 4.6 as of January 2026.
