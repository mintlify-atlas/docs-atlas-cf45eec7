This page covers the most common questions about Left-Sided Studios — how it started, how it operates, and how to get involved. If your question isn't answered here, the fastest way to reach the team is through the [Discord server](https://discord.gg/A8XMvSnkCU).

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How did Left-Sided Studios begin?">
    The studio was founded in the summer of 2023. Co-founders DaRealSansYT and RocketBlasts visited Vermetra's house for a week with no specific agenda. During that time, the three began conceptualizing a game inspired by Nintendo's Pikmin, which sparked the broader vision for Left-Sided Studios and its commitment to transparency and narrative depth. What started as an informal hangout became the founding moment of the studio.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What does the development cycle look like?">
    LSS uses an iterative and collaborative process. The studio leverages its ecosystem of subsidiary labels — including ANdE Studios and Gotch-ya Studios — to specialize across different genres while maintaining a unified creative vision. Each label focuses on what it does best (horror simulation, narrative RPGs, quirky action), and shared tooling across the studio allows the team to move resources and systems between projects efficiently. As of January 2026, all LSS projects are developed in Godot 4.6.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How can I get involved with the LSS community?">
    The community is a core part of the development process at LSS. The team is active on Discord and social media, and community feedback directly shapes the direction of active titles. The best way to get involved is to join the [Discord server](https://discord.gg/A8XMvSnkCU), where playtest sign-ups, community events, and development discussions take place.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does LSS work with other studios?">
    Yes. LSS maintains strategic alignments with partner studios to expand production capabilities. The most notable is a partnership with **Citadel Studios**, formalized in August 2025, focused on co-producing high-fidelity horror and cinematic content. LSS also operates joint production with **Skullix Media Group** for visual and audio delivery across the studio network.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What engine does LSS use?">
    Since January 2026, Left-Sided Studios has standardized on **Godot 4.6** across all active projects. Prior to this, projects were spread across Unity and Unreal Engine 4. The migration was motivated by the need for a shared codebase — allowing systems, shaders, and UI components built for one game to be reused across all LSS titles. The full rationale is detailed in the [studio blog post on the Godot migration](/news/godot-migration).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where can I play LSS games?">
    LSS publishes games on **GameJolt**. You can find the full catalog at [gamejolt.com](https://gamejolt.com) by searching for Left-Sided Studios, or follow the links on the [Projects](/projects/overview) page for direct game pages. Titles include Cardamania (in development), Bumbl, BREAKPOINT, and projects from subsidiary labels such as ANdE's Five Shifts at Fazbear's.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
