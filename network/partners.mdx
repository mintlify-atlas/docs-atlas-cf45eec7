## Citadel Studios

**Type:** Strategic Partner | **Tags:** Development, Infrastructure, Horror

**Alliance established:** August 2025

Citadel Studios entered a strategic alliance with Left-Sided Studios in August 2025 with the goal of expanding the reach and infrastructure of indie projects across both organizations. The partnership focuses on co-producing high-fidelity horror and cinematic content, with Citadel contributing development resources and infrastructure expertise that directly benefit LSS projects operating in the horror space — including ANdE Studios' *Five Shifts at Fazbear's* and Skullix Media Group's *FNaF: On the Clock*.

As a Strategic Partner, Citadel collaborates on shared development goals rather than operating under the LSS umbrella. Both studios remain independent and manage their own project pipelines, while pooling knowledge and resources on joint efforts.

## Geekza Productions

**Type:** Creative Partner | **Tags:** Indie Games, Short Films, Creative

Geekza Productions is a small studio dedicated to crafting unique indie games and compelling short films. Based in the real world, they focus on storytelling and creative expression across both digital and cinematic mediums. As a Creative Partner, Geekza collaborates with LSS on artistic and narrative projects, bringing an independent filmmaking sensibility that complements the network's growing interest in cinematic content.

The creative partnership is informal and collaborative by nature — Geekza Productions manages their own project listings on their own domain, with creative collaboration happening at the project level rather than through a formal co-production structure.
