Left-Sided Studios is more than a single studio. Under the LSS umbrella, a network of specialized labels lets each co-founder — and trusted joint ventures — pursue distinct creative visions while sharing the resources, tooling, and standards of the parent organization. The result is a multi-label ecosystem where horror fan-games, dark fantasy RPGs, cinematic series, and multiplayer experiences can all coexist and grow together.

## Organizational structure

The network flows from a single parent studio down through two tiers: the subsidiary labels that produce content, and the external partners that collaborate on shared goals.

```
Left-Sided Studios (parent)
├── Subsidiaries
│   ├── ANdE Studios           — Founder Imprint (Vermetra)
│   ├── Gotch-ya Studios       — Founder Imprint (DaRealSansYT)
│   ├── Bomb Banana Studios    — Founder Imprint (RocketBlasts)
│   ├── Skullix Media Group    — Production Unit (Joint Venture)
│   └── Endgame Studios        — Official Subsidiary
└── Partners
    ├── Citadel Studios        — Strategic Partner
    └── Geekza Productions     — Creative Partner
```

## Subsidiary types

LSS uses three distinct subsidiary classifications, each with a different scope and ownership model.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Founder Imprint">
    A Founder Imprint is a personal creative label owned and operated by one of the three LSS co-founders. These labels give founders the freedom to explore individual artistic directions — such as experimental horror or narrative RPGs — while remaining under the LSS umbrella and benefiting from shared infrastructure.

    Current Founder Imprints: **ANdE Studios** (Vermetra), **Gotch-ya Studios** (DaRealSansYT), **Bomb Banana Studios** (RocketBlasts).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Official Subsidiary">
    An Official Subsidiary is a fully integrated studio branch operating under LSS direction. Unlike Founder Imprints, these are not tied to a single founder and may grow their own teams.

    Current Official Subsidiaries: **Endgame Studios**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Production Unit">
    A Production Unit is a joint venture that handles a specific operational function for the network — in this case, media production. Production Units are not owned by a single founder.

    Current Production Units: **Skullix Media Group**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Partner types

External organizations that collaborate with LSS fall into two partnership categories.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Strategic Partner">
    Strategic partners align on development infrastructure and long-term project goals. The alliance with **Citadel Studios**, reached in August 2025, is LSS's first strategic partnership, focused on expanding the reach of indie projects and co-producing high-fidelity horror and cinematic content.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Creative Partner">
    Creative partners collaborate on artistic and storytelling output. **Geekza Productions**, a small studio making indie games and short films, operates as LSS's creative partner, bringing an independent filmmaking perspective to the ecosystem.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
