Left-Sided Studios operates five subsidiary labels: three Founder Imprints owned by each co-founder, one Production Unit joint venture, and one Official Subsidiary. Each label occupies a distinct genre space and operates with creative independence under the LSS umbrella. This page covers every label's focus, ownership, and current project portfolio.

## ANdE Studios

**Type:** Founder Imprint | **Owner:** Vermetra | **Tagline:** Experimental & Fan Projects

ANdE Studios is the personal project label of Vermetra, used to explore high-intensity simulation and experimental horror experiences. It operates within the fan-game space, producing projects inspired by established franchises with a focus on atmospheric tension and survival mechanics.

### Active projects

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Five Shifts at Fazbear's" defaultOpen>
    **Genre:** Horror / Survival | **Status:** In Development | **Planned release:** 2026

    A survival horror fan-game set at a local Fazbear Pizza Parlor. Players take on the role of a security guard navigating the dangers of the night shift. Five Shifts at Fazbear's is ANdE Studios' primary active project.

## Gotch-ya Studios

**Type:** Founder Imprint | **Owner:** DaRealSansYT | **Tagline:** Enriching RPGs

Gotch-ya Studios is the primary creative vehicle for DaRealSansYT, focusing on deep RPG systems and atmospheric dark fantasy. The label is dedicated to narrative-driven experiences where worldbuilding and character depth take center stage.

### Active projects

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Shadow of The Sanctified">
    **Genre:** RPG / Dark Fantasy | **Status:** Canceled

## Bomb Banana Studios

**Type:** Founder Imprint | **Owner:** RocketBlasts | **Tagline:** Quirky & Energetic

Bomb Banana Studios is the personal project label for RocketBlasts, bringing high energy and unique character designs to the LSS family. The label leans into vibrant, unconventional gameplay — fast-paced action wrapped in a distinctive visual identity.

### Active projects

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Eyebo" defaultOpen>
    **Genre:** Hack-and-Slash Platformer | **Status:** In Development

    Navigate a monster-filled world as a warrior eye in this action-platformer. Eyebo is Bomb Banana Studios' debut title, combining the energy of classic hack-and-slash gameplay with quirky character design.

## Skullix Media Group

**Type:** Production Unit (Joint Venture) | **Tagline:** Media & Production

Skullix Media Group is a joint venture production unit responsible for media production across the LSS network. Unlike the Founder Imprints, Skullix is not owned by a single founder — it operates as a shared resource ensuring high-quality visual and audio delivery for cinematic projects.

### Active productions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="FNaF: On the Clock" defaultOpen>
    **Format:** Cinematic Series | **Status:** In Development | **Planned premiere:** 2027

    A deep-dive cinematic series exploring the early days of Fazbear Entertainment and the founding of the iconic pizza franchise. *FNaF: On the Clock* is Skullix's flagship production and the most ambitious media project in the LSS network.

## Endgame Studios

**Type:** Official Subsidiary | **Tagline:** Innovative Gameplay

Endgame Studios is LSS's official subsidiary focused on large-scale, innovative multiplayer experiences. As an Official Subsidiary rather than a Founder Imprint, it is not tied to a single founder and is positioned for larger team collaboration as the network grows.

<Info>
  No active games are currently listed for Endgame Studios. The label is in early formation and public projects will be announced when development begins.
