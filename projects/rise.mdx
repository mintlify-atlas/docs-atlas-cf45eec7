RISE is a fast-paced roguelike platformer built around one goal: reach the top of the tower before the clock runs out. With 8 playable characters and a randomized progression system, no two runs play the same. RISE is currently in development with a release date to be announced.

## Gameplay

RISE is built on three core pillars:

- **8 playable characters** — Each character brings a distinct moveset and skill to the climb. Finding the right character for your playstyle is part of the run-to-run variety.
- **Roguelike progression** — The tower layout, pickups, and obstacles shift each run. Progression is earned through repeated attempts, with the game rewarding mastery over time.
- **Tight controls** — The game is designed with speed in mind. Controls are built to feel precise and responsive, supporting players who want to push for faster clears.

## Inspiration

RISE draws inspiration from high-speed platformers, specifically [Pizza Tower](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2231450/Pizza_Tower/) and [DIVE](https://www.roblox.com/games/102464178326906/DIVE-v0-2a). The energy and momentum of those games informed the feel and pace of RISE's movement design, while the roguelike layer adds replayability that goes beyond a pure speed-run experience.
