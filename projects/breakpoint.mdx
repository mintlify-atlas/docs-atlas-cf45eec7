BREAKPOINT is a story-driven tactical shooter that follows the last survivors of a collapsed society. Set in a decaying world, the game challenges players to make decisions that determine the fate of the remaining resistance. Development is currently **paused** while the project undergoes a full engine migration.

<Warning>
  BREAKPOINT development is currently paused. The project is being migrated from **Unreal Engine 4** to **Godot 4.6** as part of LSS's studio-wide engine consolidation. Active development will resume once the migration is complete. See the [Godot migration blog post](/news/godot-migration) for full context.
</Warning>

## Story premise

BREAKPOINT follows a group of survivors after the collapse of society. The world has decayed and the remaining resistance must navigate through it — every player decision carries consequence for the people who remain. The game puts narrative weight on tactical choices, making each encounter feel meaningful beyond the moment-to-moment gunplay.

## Features

- **Story-driven campaign** — A cinematic experience centered on character growth and the consequences of player choices. The campaign is the primary focus of the game.
- **Tactical combat** — High-stakes gunplay where positioning and strategy matter more than reflexes. Encounters are designed to reward careful planning.
- **Immersive atmosphere** — The world is built to serve the story, with environments and audio crafted to reinforce the tone of a collapsed civilization.
