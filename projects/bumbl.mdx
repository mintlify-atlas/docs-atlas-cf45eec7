Bumbl is an action-adventure strategy game set on Planet SL-3, a vibrant alien world populated by small creatures called Buggies. Players explore the planet, recruit Buggies, and use them to solve environmental puzzles and take on enemies. Release is currently TBA.

## Concept

Planet SL-3 is a colorful, living environment where the Buggies are the key to everything — each one has its own behavior and can be directed to assist in different situations. The moment-to-moment gameplay blends exploration with light strategy as you manage your Buggy squad and react to the world around you.

The original spark for Bumbl came from Nintendo's Pikmin. During the founding week of Left-Sided Studios in summer 2023, the founders began prototyping a game around the idea of commanding small creatures to interact with the world. That concept became Bumbl.
