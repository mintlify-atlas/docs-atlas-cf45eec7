## What is Left-Sided Studios?

Left-Sided Studios leverages the creative, intuitive, and unconventional side of game design to build experiences that bridge niche fan culture with polished interactive media. Since our founding in the summer of 2023, we have grown from a small hangout project into a multi-label development network spanning games, media production, and creative partnerships.
