Left-Sided Studios is an independent game development collective dedicated to building narrative-heavy action and simulation titles. Founded in 2023 by Vermetra, DaRealSansYT, and RocketBlasts, the studio operates a network of subsidiary labels to deliver unique interactive experiences across horror, RPG, strategy, and platformer genres.

## Philosophy

> We leverage the "Left Side" of the brain — our creative, intuitive, and unconventional core — to build games that bridge the gap between niche fan culture and polished interactive media.

The studio's name is a direct expression of this ethos. Every title LSS ships draws from instinct and passion first, production polish second — an approach that keeps the work experimental without sacrificing craft.

## Mission

Left-Sided Studios' mission is to deliver high-fidelity, experimental experiences that respect player agency and the spirit of the franchises that inspire us.

This guides decisions across the entire label network: from how long a project stays in development to which partnerships the studio pursues. Player agency and creative integrity are non-negotiable.

## Founding story

Here at Left-Sided Studios, we are dedicated to independent game development, sharing original games and projects on GameJolt and other platforms. Founded 2023.

The studio's soul was forged in the summer of 2023. Founders DaRealSansYT and RocketBlasts visited Vermetra's house for a week with no plan beyond gaming and messing around. The energy of those sessions produced something unexpected: a conceptual project inspired by the core mechanics of Nintendo's Pikmin. That spontaneous creative spark became the foundation of everything LSS has built since.

<Note>
  The founding quote from that summer: "We were just messing around, but we ended up conceptualizing a project inspired by the core mechanics of Pikmin. That was the moment we realized we could actually build something together." — Vermetra, Co-Founder
</Note>
